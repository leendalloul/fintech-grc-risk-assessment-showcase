# Incident Response Plan

## Roles

- **IT Manager:** technical investigation, containment, and recovery leadership
- **Compliance Officer:** regulatory assessment and evidence requirements
- **Customer Support Lead:** approved customer communications
- **External Legal Counsel:** legal and notification guidance
- **Senior Management:** business decisions and executive oversight

## Escalation

IT Manager → Compliance Officer → Legal Counsel → Senior Management

## Preparation

- Maintain security logs and monitoring.
- Document playbooks and contact lists.
- Prepare approved customer and regulator communication templates.
- Assign incident roles before an event occurs.

## Detection and Triage

Potential indicators include unusual login activity, abnormal data transfers, and spikes in failed authentication. Events are classified as Low, Medium, High, or Critical according to data impact, operational impact, scope, and confidence.

## Containment, Eradication, and Recovery

- Disable compromised access and isolate affected systems.
- Preserve logs and forensic evidence.
- Determine and remove the root cause.
- Reset affected credentials and remediate control weaknesses.
- Restore from verified backups and monitor carefully.
- Resume services gradually after validation.

## Post-Incident Review

Document the incident summary, timeline, root cause, impact, response actions, lessons learned, and assigned corrective actions. Feed findings back into the risk register and control-improvement program.
